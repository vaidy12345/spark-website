Case Study

Battle-tested in the hardest environment.

How one high-volume tutor saved 25+ hours per video and increased student engagement.

The Context

A technical tutor running daily live classes via Zoom. The content was rich but unstructured—often 2 hours of dense problem-solving, with cross-talk from students and variable audio quality.

The Problem

Students were asking for recordings, but raw 2-hour video files were unusable for revision. To make them useful, the tutor was spending his entire weekend manually splitting videos, writing timestamps, and creating PDF notes. He was burning out.

Before Spark: "I was spending more time editing than teaching."

The Solution

We deployed Spark's 100+ step automation pipeline. The tutor simply dragged the raw Zoom file into the dashboard.

Spark automatically:

  • Transcribed the messy audio with high accuracy.
  • Identified logical chapter breaks (e.g., "Problem 1: Flux Calculation").
  • Removed 15 minutes of dead air and setup time.
  • Generated a searchable concept map and study notes.
  • Created a quiz for each chapter to test understanding.

The Results

The outcome was immediate. The "grind" disappeared.
