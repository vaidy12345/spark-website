Access to teacher dashboards, quiz results, and tutoring tools is protected using signed tokens and role-based permissions. Students only see what they've been invited to; teachers and admins have a broader view, but still within the boundaries of their own organization. The philosophy behind Spark is to stop and surface issues rather than quietly guessing.
