Understand the product

Spark starts from the teaching you already do — on Zoom, YouTube, or your LMS — and turns it into something students can actually move through: chapters, notes, quizzes, and a tutor that stays inside your content. Modern learners are juggling short attention spans, multi-tasking, and content overload, so Spark structures everything as short, focused micro-learning units instead of overwhelming 90-minute walls of video.

  • check_circle

    Already running on 1,000+ videos and 2,000+ hours of real teaching.

  • check_circle

    Battle-tested with 100+ students in high-stakes math and science prep — and works just as well for adult learners.

  • Takes in long recordings and finds natural breakpoints for chapters and micro-sections.
  • Generates structured study notes in your voice instead of a generic summary.
  • Builds chapter-level quizzes and a per-course tutor from the same source material.
  • Reshapes long lectures into short, focused micro-learning units so learners can make progress in 5–10 minute bursts.

Safety and ownership

Spark is opinionated about what the AI is allowed to say. It stays grounded in your content, and your recordings — and everything derived from them — stay yours.

  • verified

    Running on real group classes since June 2025, powering daily live sessions taught over Zoom.

  • security

    Built by the same team that has safely handled years of payments and student data on PracticeNow.

  • The tutor answers from your lectures and notes — not from the open web.
  • When the answer isn't in the course, Spark would rather block the answer than guess.
  • We don't train models on your videos; they are only used to ground answers.
