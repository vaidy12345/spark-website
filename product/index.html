Safety and ownership

Spark's tutor answers from your lectures and notes - not from the open web - and when the answer isn't in the course, it would rather block than guess.

Your videos are never used to train models; they only ground answers.

And all the derived content - notes, quizzes, and reports - stays yours and is downloadable.
