Types of Teachers

Built for four types of subject matter experts.

Whether you teach online, at a university, within an enterprise, or build an audience—Spark adapts to how you already work. Each role uses different features to achieve different goals.

school

High School Tutors

Online tutors teaching math, science, and other subjects to middle and high school students. Often complementing live Zoom classes with recorded content.

Monetization: Standard feature

Students typically pay subscriptions for access to courses and live classes.

Key Features:

account_tree

Course Structure

Turn long Zoom recordings into chaptered courses with micro-sections for easy revision.

note_alt

Study Notes with Formula Rendering

Accurate study notes with proper formula rendering—critical for STEM subjects.

quiz

Quizzes (Multiple Difficulty Levels)

Generate quizzes per-video or across videos, with Bloom's taxonomy-aligned difficulty levels.

insights

Live Quiz Reports

Real-time insights during live classes showing which students struggle and which concepts need review.

verified_user

Granular Access Control

Control access by grade, subject, and subscription—8th graders only see 8th grade content, etc.

forum

AI Tutor

Course-grounded AI tutor answers student questions instantly, reducing email support.

search

Semantic Search

Students find specific topics across hundreds of hours of content instantly.

account_circle

University Professors

Full-time or visiting faculty at universities teaching undergraduate or graduate courses. May create content for distance learning programs or supplementary resources.

Monetization: May be useful

Universities may charge for distance programs or supplementary courses. Professors may not monetize directly.

Key Features:

account_tree

Structured Course Organization

Organize lecture recordings into semester-long courses with clear chapter structure.

note_alt

Comprehensive Study Notes

Generate detailed notes from lectures for students to review complex academic content.

quiz

Assessment Generation

Create quizzes and assessments aligned with course objectives and Bloom's taxonomy.

trending_up

Student Progress Tracking

Monitor which concepts students struggle with to adjust teaching approach.

verified_user

Access Control by Enrollment

Ensure only enrolled students access course content, with LDAP/SSO support for enterprise.

rule

Accuracy & Hallucination Control

Critical for academic content—ensures study notes and AI tutor stay within lecture boundaries.

business

Enterprise Domain Experts

Senior professionals teaching internal training, onboarding, or upskilling content for employees within their organization. May work for consultancies, tech companies, or specialized training firms.

Monetization: Typically internal

Employees typically don't pay for training. Access control by role/department is more important than payments.

Key Features:

account_tree

Modular Course Structure

Break down training content into digestible modules for busy professionals.

verified_user

Role-Based Access Control (LDAP/SSO)

Integrate with enterprise systems to control access by employee role, department, or clearance level.

category

Segments for Targeting

Segment employees by role, department, or training completion to target specific content.

trending_up

Completion & Progress Tracking

Monitor which employees complete training and identify knowledge gaps for follow-up.

forum

AI Tutor for On-Demand Support

Provide instant answers to employee questions about training content without scheduling sessions.

fact_check

Content Ownership & Privacy

Enterprise-grade content ownership and privacy controls—content stays within the organization.

videocam

Independent Coaches

YouTube educators, online coaches, and content creators teaching skills, hobbies, or professional development. Build audiences and monetize through courses, memberships, or live sessions.

Monetization: Standard feature

Primary use case—turn existing YouTube content or live sessions into paid courses with subscriptions and memberships.

Key Features:

storefront

Unified Storefront

One portal for courses, live classes, and recordings—students discover, buy, and learn in one place.

payments

Flexible Pricing & Plans

Subscriptions, fixed-duration programs, bundles, and country-wise pricing for global audiences.

public

Country-Wise Pricing

Automatically show localized prices based on student location for global reach.

account_tree

Course Structure from YouTube

Import existing YouTube videos or Zoom recordings and automatically structure into courses.

redeem

Discount Codes & Promotions

Run promotions, launch discounts, and manage marketing campaigns directly in Spark.

monitoring

Student Engagement Reports

See which content resonates, track completion rates, and identify opportunities for new courses.

forum

AI Tutor Reduces Support Load

Course-grounded AI answers common questions, freeing time to focus on content creation.
